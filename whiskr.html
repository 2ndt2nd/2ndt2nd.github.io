Introduction
WHISKR is a mobile application catering to first-time cat owners in adapting to their new lifestyle as their pet's caretaker. Our goal is to relieve the anxiety of new cat owners by addressing knowledge gaps and enabling their confidence to practice good habits.

problem rationale

TARGET AUDIENCE

Newer cat owners frequently attempt to over-prepare to enter their pet-parenthood, yet they frequently experience anxiety due to the uncertainties during the early stages.

We interviewed

  • ▌ 3 Potential Adopters
  • ▌ 4 Cat Owners
  • ▌ 2 Foster Caretakers

And Found That

  • ▌ Ongoing support post-adoption helps in adjusting to new environments
  • ▌ Reliable pre-adoption information is crucial for reducing return rates
  • ▌ An educational campaign can bridge the knowledge gap for new adopters

problem exploration process

user journeys and personas

These brief representations of the target user helps to narrow down the specific type of people that we design for. With that in mind, I developed the in depth timeframes elaborating common experience of learning how to raise cats.

Solution Forming Posters
DESIGN INTERVENTION

Design ideas are presented as posters, highlighting the target demographic, overview of the design solution, and proposed value in the intervention.

solution forming

Idea Storyboard

The concept for WHISKR was built on this idea of combining actionable prompts a personalized experience. This storyboard was created to decide our development direction and plan the next tasks, which includes developing the proposed features and creating a suitable mockup.

Alternative Ideas

I proposed another design solution in the form of a board game so that inexperienced players can face scenarios derived from real cat-caretaking. Despite being sidelined for the journey app, we integrated ideas such as community building and filling in knowledge gaps.

key interface screens

These are the key screens that allow the user to accomplish their tasks to guide the user through simple interfaces and guided actions. The menus and information are presented with a friendly and intuitive look.

Personalized Onboarding

At the start of the user's "interactive journey", they can create a specific a profile for their cat. These details become more important for differences such as between cat breeds and circumstances.

Daily Tasks

Keeping caretaking chores as regular tasks helps to keep cat-owners accountable and track habits regularly. The tasks prompt the users with actionable checklists with helpful tips and sub-tasks for larger tasks.

Owner Achievements

Being able to look back to see your progress as a caretaker gives a sense of accomplishment. At the same time, newer owners can find other people possibly in the local community to help their own journey.
