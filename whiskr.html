Introduction
WHISKR is a mobile application catering to first-time cat owners in adapting to their new lifestyle as their pet's caretaker. The proposed design is meant to tackle problems for specific target audiences, which in this this case are people who wants to take care of cats.
problem rationale
TARGET AUDIENCE

Newer cat owners frequently attempt to over-prepare to enter their pet-parenthood, yet they frequently experience anxiety due to the uncertainties during the early stages.

DESIGN GOALS
Ensuring Confidence in Cat Parenting

We seek to tackle the initial anxiety and uncertainties that first-time owners have. By providing an easy to track routine, they can be ensured to be reminded about their cat's needs.

Reducing Knowledge Gaps

Rather than having to look up everything themselves, our team hopes to bring the answers in one tidy package. With all the key knowledge in one place, the entry to cat-parenthood can be made significantly easier.

Ensuring Confidence in Cat Parenting

We design the solution to not only fill in the unknowns, but also change the user's behavior themselves. The goal here is to combine learning and doing into one cohesive experience which makes it easy to remember.

user journeys and personas

These brief representations of the target user helps to narrow down the specific type of people that we design for. With that in mind, I personally developed the in depth timeframes elaborating common experience of learning how to raise cats.

Solution Forming Posters
Design Intervention

Design ideas are presented as posters, highlighting the target demographic, overview of the design solution, and proposed value in the intervention.

solution forming
App Storyboard

The concept for WHISKR was built on this idea of combining actionable prompts and forming communities. Creating a storyboard helps to solidify the concept and address possible issue at an earlier stage.

Game Storyboard

The notion behind the boardgame is to experience cat-caretaking for the first time as a lighthearted game. The gameplay allows players to face scenarios which cat owners face day-to day.

key interface screens
These are the key screens that allow the user to accomplish their tasks to guide the user through simple interfaces and guided actions. The menus and information are presented with a friendly and intuitive look.
Personalized Onboarding

At the start of the user's "interactive journey", they can create a specific a profile for their cat. These details become more important for differences such as between cat breeds and circumstances.

Daily Tasks

Keeping caretaking chores as regular tasks helps to keep cat-owners accountable and track habits regularly. The tasks prompt the users with actionable checklists with helpful tips and sub-tasks for larger tasks.

Owner Achievements
Being able to look back to see your progress as a caretaker gives a sense of accomplishment. At the same time, newer owners can find other people possibly in the local community to help their own journey.
