About Me

I am a graphic designer focusing on UI/UX studying at Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology with a great interest in designing visual directions. I enjoy creating visual experiences in digital and mixed media projects with experimental designs derived from specialized solutions.

I frequently gather inspiration from professional works, as well as my personal interests and hobbies. I believe that including the inclusion of unconventional references enriches the capability to expand the possibilities within my decision making.
