Overview

This project is a business-to-business focused explainer video Aquapress Pro dry disinfectant tablets. The animation elaborates the product's unique benefits and use cases compared to common alternatives in the Indonesian market to garner the interest of potential business partners and clients.

Project Planning

Creative Framework

Facing a large scale project demands clear planning. Following the structure of moodboards, storyboards, and multiple draft stages results in timely progress during the development process with the added bonus of better estimates for deliverables.

moodboards and storyboards

Assisting the client with conveying their creative vision demands well-informed suggestions. I compiled a collection of moodboards were compiled to assist in visualizing the client's intended direction. Beginning from one of the studio's project which the client took interest in. Following the moodboard, I used still designs and sketches to create preliminary visuals to lay out the narrative.

CREATING ANIMATION

Integrating 3D Elements

I used 3D animation to create elements for the video and demonstrate the product's real use cases, leveraging the potential for strong depictions. Using flat shaders helped with compositing 2D and 3D elements to appear visually cohesive, which is appropriate for the animation.

animation compositing

The video needs to be rich yet concise to ensure the interest and attention of potential partners. During this stage, prior ideas and storyboard contents are frequently challenged to improve on each iteration, more often looking vastly different than their initial concepts. In the end, my rapid iteration proved to be essential for gaining improvements beyond all the starting ideas.

Project Reflection

skills learned

Completing an independent motion graphics video production process significantly improved my technical consistency and creative expertise. This experience further polished my prior capabilities, all without neglecting essential soft skills in working with other parties in a real project.

as a developing professional

Working in a creative studio brought many industry insights and exposure to professional settings. Thanks to the studio team's support, I learned about managing projects from start to finish and working with clients more effectively.
