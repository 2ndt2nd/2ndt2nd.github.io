Introduction
The project aims to develop an art direction for Klanggg Festival, which will be used to create an interactive landing page with an emphasis on visual communication and user experience. During the project I took charge as the art direction lead during this five-week project by organizing design research and exploring new visual directions.
Designer inspiration study
DAN FRIEDMAN

Dan Friedman is a notable figure in graphic design who followed Wolfgang Weingart and Armin Hoffman. He was a figure who influenced post-modern design and new wave typography.

The project's art direction is inspired by from Friedman's works and analyzed how his design differs from the Swiss Style in effort to explore experimental designs.

Opposing Tension
The use of elements at opposing ends of the composition creates strong figure-ground relation. The visuals come together as a cohesive whole and creates a larger combined form with strong visual draw.
layered Collage
The overlapping of elements leads to a distinct hierarchy between multiple points of interest. When executed properly, the composition becomes rich and layered with pronounced spatial depth.
textural contrasts
Utilizing dithered textures  created unique figure-ground relations and adding more draw to the textured visuals. Textures can also imitate transparency, thus contributing to hierarchies.

VISUAL Assets Development

VISUAL LANGUAGE BREAKDOWN
Without being able to identify what makes a visual direction great, the spark that created the initial design is not much more than a happy accident. Anchoring the design elements through words helps to rationalize creative design. Through this grounded rationale, the visual cohesion within the project is maintained.
Graphic Assets

USER FLOW DEMONSTRATION
collage style navigation

The collage present in the landing page provides easy navigation to access the opera shows' pages. With a hover interaction, one of the four options are highlighted and provided with a brief description. Clicking on the option then opens the page for the individual opera shows.

secondary navigation

The provided menu component at the bottom could be hovered on to reveal a quick access menu as an alternative to the main navigation. This secondary navigation would be expanded to include pages such as the venue information, ticket buying and other relevant pages.

curated content

Each individual opera show's pages contains tailored content to compliment the opera experience. Adding context provides a helpful introduction to the context, which both garner interest for the current Klanggg Festival and upcoming operas in the future.
