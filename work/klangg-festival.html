Introduction
The project aims to develop an art direction for Klanggg Festival, which will be used to create an interactive landing page with an emphasis on visual communication and user experience. During the project I took charge as the art direction lead during this five-week project by organizing design research and exploring new visual directions.
The project is one of my first large-scale projects involving multimedia design which created a great learning opportunity to create a project which attracts attention and maintain visual consistency, while continuing to iterate upon previous successes and exploration.
Designer inspiration study
analyzing professional works
DAN FRIEDMAN
Dan Friedman is a notable figure in graphic design who followed Wolfgang Weingart and Armin Hoffman in developing the postmodern design and new wave typography.
The project's art direction is inspired by from Friedman's works and analyzed how his design differs from the Swiss Style in effort to explore experimental designs.
Opposing Tension
design principle
The use of elements at opposing ends of the composition creates strong figure-ground relation. The visuals come together as a cohesive whole and creates a larger combined form with strong visual draw.
layered Collage
design principle
The overlapping of elements leads to a distinct hierarchy between multiple points of interest. When executed properly, the composition becomes rich and layered with pronounced spatial depth.
textural contrasts
design principle
Utilizing dithered textures  created unique figure-ground relations and adding more draw to the textured visuals. Textures can also imitate transparency, thus contributing to hierarchies.

Initial Posters
Several visual directions were implemented in the form of poster to create a visual language with the previously explored elements. The potential of each exploration line are put to the test through iterations of the posters and their flexibility to take different arrangements in multiple form factors.
Without being able to identify what makes a visual direction great, the spark that created the initial design is not much more than a happy accident.


Anchoring the design elements through words helps to rationalize creative design. Through this grounded rationale, the visual cohesion within the project is maintained.
Graphic Assets
The employed visual grammar is used as graphic assets as an advertising board, a mobile billboard truck, and an event ticket.

These assets indicate that the design direction shows promise as a visual direction for the landing page project.
