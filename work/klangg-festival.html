INTRODUCTION
The project aims to develop an art direction for Klanggg Festival, which will be used to create an interactive landing page with an emphasis on visual communication and user experience. I took charge as the art direction lead during this five-week project by organizing design research and exploring new visual directions.
Designer inspiration study
analyzing professional works
DAN FRIEDMAN
Dan Friedman is a notable figure in graphic design who followed Wolfgang Weingart and Armin Hoffman in developing the postmodern design and new wave typography.
We took inspiration from Friedman's works and analyzed how his design differs from the International Typographic Style in effort to explore experimental design styles.
Opposing Tension
visual direction
The use of elements at oppposing ends of the composition creates strong figure-ground relation. The visuals come together as a cohesive whole and creates a larger combined form with strong visual draw.
Overlapping Collage
visual direction
The overlapping of elements leads to a distinct hierarchy between multiple points of interest. When executed properly, the composition becomes rich and layered.

Developing Visuals
